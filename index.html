How to play

  • Move — W A S D or arrow keys
  • Look — click and drag; scroll to zoom
  • Interact — E (operate a station, talk, take an item)
  • Jump — Space
  • Travel — T cycles districts; use portals for the story route
  • Sound — M  ·  Save — F5  ·  Help — H
